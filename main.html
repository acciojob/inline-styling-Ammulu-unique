Flowers photos

Flowers are the reproductive structures of angiosperms, designed to produce seeds via pollination, often facilitated by animals or wind. They consist of key parts like stamens (male) and pistils (female), often surrounded by colorful petals to attract pollinators. There are over 250,000 to 400,000 species, with lifecycles ranging from annuals to perennials.

Roses pictures

Roses are perennial flowering shrubs and vines in the genus Rosa and the family Rosaceae, featuring over 100 species and thousands of hybrids. Known for their fragrant, colorful flowers (white, yellow, pink, red, maroon) and sharp thorns, they are popular ornamental plants that thrive in sunny, well-drained soil.
